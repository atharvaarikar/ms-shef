how to make paneer butter masala: 
firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil,
 2 tbsp butter,
 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli.
 now add 1 onion and saute until onions turn and golden brown.
 keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 
1 tsp chilli powder,
 ¾ tsp coriander powder, 
¼ tsp cumin powder,
½ garam masala and 
1 tsp salt.
